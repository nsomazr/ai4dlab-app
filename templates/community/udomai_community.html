About the Community

UDOM AI Community is a Student-led community founded 2018 at The University of Dodoma focusing on developing skills in Data Science , Machine Learning , Artificial intelligence . Focusing in solving local problems using Artificial intelligence and other data related technologies.

UDOM AI Community was established in the year 2018 by the group of students from the College of Informatics and Virtual Education at The University of Dodoma, since it's establishement community has been able to engage in different activities such as IndabaX Tanzania 2019 and UmojaHack 2020, 2021 and 2022.
