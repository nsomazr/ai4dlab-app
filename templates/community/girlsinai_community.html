About Girls in AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is becoming an integral element of information and communication technologies (ICTs), from everyday digital tools to advanced supplicated technologies. It is shaping every corner of our lives from healthcare to finance, from education to government. Despite these benefits, AI is still a very novice concept in many parts of the developing countries such as Tanzania. In order for AI to gain traction, much awareness is needed to shed light on the potential of AI for community development.

Against this background, AI4D Labs Anglophone a conglomerate project of UDOM and Nelson Mandela Institute in partnership with DarasaTech saw it fit to undertake awareness workshops to university students to showcase the potential of AI in solving community challenges.

Ojectives
